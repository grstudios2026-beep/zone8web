Features

Everything you need
to race smarter

Built specifically for functional fitness racing — not a generic fitness app.

⏱️
Live Split Tracking
Track every station in real time — SkiErg, Sled Push, Wall Balls and all 8 stations. See your exact splits the moment you cross the line.
📊
Race Analytics
Compare every race, identify your weakest station, and track improvement over time. Know exactly where you're losing seconds.
🎙️
Voice Coach
Real-time audio coaching during your race. Choose your personality — Normal, Military, or Karen. Your call.
🏆
Leagues
Create or join competitive leagues. Race against friends with live leaderboards. Singles, doubles, relay — challenge formats included.
🏋️
Smart Planner
Generate personalised race prep plans based on your weak stations. Save them directly to your training schedule.
📋
Coach Mode
Coaches can manage athletes, assign weekly training plans, and monitor race history — all inside one app.
