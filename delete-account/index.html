Delete Your Zone 8 Account

You can delete your Zone 8 account and all associated data at any time, either directly in the app or by contacting us.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open the Zone 8 app and log in.
  2. Go to Profile (bottom right tab).
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon).
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you cannot access the app, send an email to guillaumeribeiro92@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, email, age, weight, height, photo)
  • All workout and race history
  • Feed posts, comments, and likes
  • League memberships and results
  • Training plans
  • Consent records and push notification tokens
  • All device-local data (cleared immediately on deletion)
Note on subscriptions: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your App Store or Google Play subscription. Please cancel your subscription separately in your device's subscription settings before or after deleting your account to avoid future charges.

Data retention after deletion

All personal data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days of the deletion request. Anonymised, aggregated statistics that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for service improvement purposes.
