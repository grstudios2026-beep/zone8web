You can delete your Zone8 account and all associated data at any time, directly in the app or by contacting us.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

  1. Open the Zone8 app and log in.
  2. Go to Profile (bottom right tab).
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon, top right).
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm when prompted. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you cannot access the app, email zone8privacy@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and the email address linked to your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Profile — name, email, age, weight, height, photo
  • Race history — all workout and split data
  • Social content — feed posts, comments, and likes
  • League data — memberships and results
  • Training plans — all coach plans and custom exercises
  • Consent records — GDPR consent timestamps
  • Push tokens — device notification tokens

All device-local data is erased immediately. Server data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your App Store or Google Play subscription. Cancel your subscription in your device's subscription settings separately to avoid future charges.

Partial data deletion

You can also delete specific content without deleting your account: remove individual posts in the Feed, delete race results in History, or clear your profile photo in Settings. For other specific data removal requests, contact zone8privacy@gmail.com.
